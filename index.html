Hindfoot Alignment Comparator

Compare radiographic hindfoot alignment angle with smartphone posterior heel photo alignment

Manual point-click hindfoot alignment measurement

Upload a hindfoot alignment radiograph and a posterior standing clinical photo. Click four landmarks on each image to calculate angles, compare results, and export training-ready labels.

Point-click workflow

Radiograph HAA

  1. Click proximal tibial axis point.
  2. Click distal tibial axis point.
  3. Click tibial axis / joint-line intersection point.
  4. Click most inferior calcaneus point.

Clinical photo angle

  1. Click proximal leg/Achilles midline point.
  2. Click distal leg/Achilles midline point.
  3. Click heel-axis proximal point near posterior ankle.
  4. Click inferior/posterior heel center.
Dataset-first design: Every finished measurement can export JSON labels with landmark coordinates, image dimensions, angles, and metadata for future machine-learning training.
